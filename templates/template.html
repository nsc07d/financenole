Paying Off Loans:

You've indicated that you have a loan that has a rate of {{ apr }}%. This is the next thing that you need to tackle. Every month that you have to continue to pay off just interest rate is money quite literally going the toilet. The quicker you can pay this off the better.

There are two ways to pay off this debt.
  • The Avalance method, debts are paid down in order of interest rate, starting with the debt that carries the highest interest rate. This is the financially optimal method of paying down debt, and you will pay less money overall compared to the snowball method.

  • The Snowball method, popularized by Dave Ramsey, debts are paid down in order of balance size, starting with the smallest. Paying off small debts first may give you a psychological boost and improve one's cash flow situation, as paid off debts free up minimum payments. The downside is that larger loans (that may be at higher interest rates) are left untouched for longer, costing more in the long run.


These methods are the best way to go about reducing your large. If you continue to have problems please consult a professional, licensed financial planner
